Easy and healthy breakfast recipe

MASCARPONE & BERRIES TOAST


Creamy mascarpone is delightful when topped with mixed berries and mint for this bright-tasting,easy and healthy breakfast toast.

  • DURATION TO COOK: 5 Minutes

  • NUTRITION PROFILE:


    • Low Carbohydrates
    Egg free
    Vegetarian
    Low Sodium
    Nut free
    Soy-Free
    Low Added Sugar

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Slice whole grain bread,toasted
  • 2 tablespoon mascarpone cheese
  • Half cup berries, such as raspberries, blueberries or chopped strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon mint leaves

    DIRECTIONS:


    STEP 1: Top toast with mascarpone, berries and mint.

    EXCHANGES:

    5 fat, 1 starch, 1 fruit.
